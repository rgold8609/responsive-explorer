How to animate!

And now, let's get to the whole reason you're here. Learning the lost art of hand-drawn animation (as in 2-D, not on paper but that works too, I guess). But before that, I have a bit of a confession to make.

One Small Issue

I don't know how to draw.

Ok, that may be a bit too far but it's true I'm no artist. I draw recreationally but I'm really not great at it. But that's what's great about animation. You don't have to be a great artist to learn the basics. It helps, yeah, but learning animation and drawing at the same time like me still works. And since we're learning the basics here (just look at what section of the website you're in, it doesn't get more cut and dry than that), we're going over simple techniques that all animators use.

PLEASE NOTE:

This website will continue to be updated with useful tips. And if you have a tip you don't see here, please share it with me so I can add it. You can find my contact info under "contact" (obviously). It would be greatly appreciated :) Now, enjoy!
